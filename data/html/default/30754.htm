Watcher of Antharas Gilmore:
I am Gilmore, watcher of the beast Antharas...
I must monitor every breath that demon dragon takes, every twitch of its mighty tail, to assure that it does not awaken from its deep sleep. I have watched the beast for 1,000 years, since I led an army against him. We were able to subdue the beast, but he must be watched.
Do you truly desire to face Antharas, the Earth Dragon? He is imprisoned in a cave with no entryway. There is only one way in or out... The Heart of Warding...
