Aria Furstmater:
Friends, are you? Trusted allies? You are wise.
If only I had known. Long have I searched for answers, power. Many a powerful foe fell before me, but yet my love remains imprisoned inside a seal, and I am powerless.
What is the purpose of all the knowledge I gathered along the way? All the wealth, the power? It is all to naught.
Help me. Slay the foul beasts that haunt me, and I will reward you handsomely.
