Fantasy Isle Paddies:
A Power Shirt can be enchanted by Scroll: Enchant Power Shirts.

The Power Shirt can be safely enchanted up to +3, and can be enchanted to a maximum of +10. Additionally, if you successfully enchant the Power Shirt to +4 or higher, the following attributes are added:
+4: CON +2.
+5: CON and MEN +2, Speed +7.
+6: STR,INT,CON and MEN +2,      Speed +7.
+7: STR,INT,CON and MEN +2,      Speed +7, P. Skill damage +93,      Skill MP consumption -3%,      Active skill: Blessed Escape.
+8: STR,INT,CON,MEN,WIT and DEX +2,      Speed +7, P. Skill damage +93,      Skill MP consumption -3%,      P. Critical Damage +41.3,      M. Critical Rate +2,      Active skill: Blessed Escape.
+9:STR,INT,WIT and DEX +2,      CON and MEN +3,      Speed +7, P. Skill damage +93,      Skill MP consumption -3%,      P. Critical Damage +41.3,      M. Critical Rate +2,      Active skill: Invincibility.
+10: WIT and DEX +2,      STR,INT,CON and MEN +3,      Speed +7, P. Skill damage +93,      Skill MP consumption -3%,      P. Critical Damage +41.3,      M. Critical Rate +2,      Active skill: Invincibility.
