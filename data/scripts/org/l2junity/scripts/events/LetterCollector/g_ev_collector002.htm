Angel Cat:
How to Participate in the Event:
If you hunt monsters in the Lineage II world, you have a chance of obtaining letter-engraved items. If you collect these letters and bring them to me, I will give you a gift depending on the combination.
There are a total of 16 letters, and they are: A, C, E, F, G, I, L, N, O, S, T, II, Y, R, H, and M.
These letters can be assembled to make "LINEAGEII", "NCSOFT", or "HARMONY". You receive a different gift for each combination. Also, the kind of gift you receive also depends on chance.
Gift Guide:
If you collect "LINEAGEII", you can receive the following gifts: - Baium's Ring - Blessed Scroll (R1/R2/R95/R99) ; Unbind Scroll (R/R95/R99) - Rare Soul Crystal Pack (R/R95/R99) - Hair Accessory Enchant Scroll & Life Stone - Gemstone (A/S/R) - Scroll of Escape: Village Box - Superior Quick Healing Potion - Letter Collector's Gift (8th Anniversary)
If you collect "NCSOFT", you can receive the following gifts: - Queen Ant's Ring - Blessed Enchant Scrolls & Scrolls of Destruction (S/R) - Rare Soul Crystal Pack (R/R95/R99) - Attribute Crystal- Gemstone (A/S/R) - Scroll of Escape: Village - Superior Quick Healing Potion - Letter Collector's Gift (8th Anniversary)
If you collect "HARMONY", you can receive the following gifts: - Queen Ant's Ring or Baium's Ring - Giant & Ancient & Legendary Dye (Lv. 2 - 5) - Rare Soul Crystal Pack (R/R95/R99) - Gemstone (A/S/R)- Scroll of Escape: Village Box - Highest Power Temporary Healing Potion - Letter Collector's Gift (8th Anniversary)
