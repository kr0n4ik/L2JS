Asamah:
First let me tell you who Shilen once was.
Untold ages ago, the union of Einhasad the Goddess of Creation, and Gran Kain the God of Destruction, produced 5 God-Children. Shilen was first-born, and with each day that passed she grew wiser, more courageous, more beautiful and more skilled.
Loved by her subjects, she was the equal of her brothers when it came to commanding an army, an expert in both strategy and tactics.
