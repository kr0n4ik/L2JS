Asamah:
Einhasad and Gran Kain watched their 5 children grow, assigning each a duty suited to their individual abilities and strengths. Two of the God-Children were harder to pin down, though.
One was Shilen, whose many talents made any individual duty seem too small. The other was Eva, whose quiet, passive nature seemed to obscure any talent she might have had. Easily swayed by her siblings, she seemed to have no real will of her own.
