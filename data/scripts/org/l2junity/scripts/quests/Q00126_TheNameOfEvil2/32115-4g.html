Asamah:
Shilen commanded my Elrokian ancestors to rule over the dinosaurs, using powerful magic to control and restrain them. It was the peak of Shilen's glory, and the world owed her a debt it could never repay.
But the other gods envied Shilen, and sought a chance to discredit her. At last she was seduced by her father, Gran Kain. When Einhasad her mother found out, she flew into a rage and punished Shilen, who by then was great with child.
